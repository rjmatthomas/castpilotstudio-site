Audition command center for actors

Prepare faster, rehearse smarter, and keep every audition organized.

CastPilot Studio is a mobile app for actors that helps organize audition emails, sides, deadlines, self-tape instructions, and role details so performers can move from audition notice to focused prep.

Audition import

Connect Gmail with read-only access to find audition-related emails from casting platforms, agents, and managers.

AI prep

Turn sides and audition details into method-aware preparation, character notes, research prompts, and rehearsal ideas.

Rehearsal tools

Practice lines, organize notes, build self-tape checklists, and keep each audition's next steps in one place.

Google data use

CastPilot Studio requests Gmail read-only access only after user consent and only to provide user-facing audition import and audition prep features. The app does not send, delete, or modify Gmail messages and does not sell Gmail data or use it for advertising.
